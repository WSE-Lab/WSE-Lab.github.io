Research Overview

Dynamically Generating Critical Scenarios via Natural Language with Configurable Strategies to Uncover Decision Flaws in Autonomous Driving Systems.

LiveTCM

LiveTCM is an adaptive testing framework for Autonomous Driving Systems that integrates restricted natural language processing with model-driven techniques.

It dynamically generates, executes, and verifies test case specifications through configurable environment strategies such as DeepCollision-optimized DQN or Random, enabling rigorous validation of ADS algorithms including InterFuser and the default algorithm of CARLA.
